## Voice & Tone

**The voice is:** Direct. Thoughtful. Grounded. Slightly formal but never stiff.

**Think:** A smart founder writing a long-form essay — not a startup pitch deck, not a casual tweet.

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Write in first person | Use corporate "we" language |
| Short, declarative sentences | Fluffy adjective stacks |
| Specific, concrete details | Vague claims ("passionate about innovation") |
| Honest about what's being built | Overpromise or hype |
| European register (measured, precise) | Silicon Valley energy (move fast, crush it) |

**Examples:**

✅ "I build AI automation systems for service businesses. The goal is to replace repetitive revenue work with systems that run without you."

❌ "I'm passionate about leveraging cutting-edge AI to revolutionize the way businesses scale their operations."

✅ "Simine Paris is a gallery with a focused thesis: Iranian contemporary art deserves a permanent presence in Europe's conversation."

❌ "We showcase amazing Iranian artists to connect cultures and celebrate diversity."

### Blog (`/blog`)

**Goal:** Writing index. Essays, notes, experiments. No casual tweets.

**Content types:**
- Long-form essays on AI, automation, business systems
- Gallery exhibition notes and artist reflections
- Experiment logs from building products
- Notes on tools, workflows, and processes

**Post frontmatter schema:**
```mdx
---
title: "Post Title"
date: "2025-03-15"
category: "AI" | "Art" | "Business" | "Tools" | "Personal"
description: "One sentence description for the index."
draft: false
---
```

**Writing rules for blog:**
- No listicles with emoji bullets
- Paragraphs of 3–5 sentences
- Headers sparingly — only when sections genuinely shift
- End posts with a clear conclusion, not "thanks for reading"

## Content Rules

1. **No lorem ipsum ever.** Use placeholder copy from this document.
2. **Dates:** Format as "March 2025" or "15 March 2025" — never "03/15/25".
3. **Company names:** Always "KaavOps" (not "Kaavops" or "KAAVOPS") and "Simine Paris" (not "simine paris").
4. **Language:** English only for v1.
5. **Links:** All external links open in `_blank` with `rel="noopener noreferrer"`.
6. **Em dashes** (—) are used throughout the site as a visual signature. Use them in copy naturally.
